## Branding for your chapter

Please read and follow the instruction on our [Branding Guidelines](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1m_IiX0JZCi5RIiOPWCi--zd8ZoVWA6oY/) to ensure that your chapter's branding is consistent with the RLadies+ Global brand.

Here are some highlights and links to the most important resources:

### Fonts

The main font to be used in all text is **Poppins**. [You can download from Google Fonts.](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Poppins)
For accessibility, make sure the line spacing of all text is at least 1.5 pt.
The hierarchy of the text helps with readability and accessibility. The main title should be _Poppins Medium_ and the Headings of different level _Poppins Semibold_. The main text should be _Poppins Regular_. For emphasis, you can use _Poppins Bold_.

### Logo

The logo of R-Ladies can be found in the
[branding-materials](https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/1s60cO3zRkpa-dnADPQj6w2Z1iemXH98p).

## Templates

We have a [series of templates](https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/1RLGLfUqJF1qBeduJPADM2j8YnFUosDvi) for you to use for your chapter's events, social media, etc. to make sure that the branding is consistent across the organization.

You can of course create your own template and share it, but feel free to re-use existing ones as much as possible to save time and ensure consistency.
