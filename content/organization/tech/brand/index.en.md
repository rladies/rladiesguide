## Branding for your chapter

Please read and follow the instruction on our [Branding Guidelines](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1m_IiX0JZCi5RIiOPWCi--zd8ZoVWA6oY/) to ensure that your chapter's branding is consistent with the RLadies+ Global brand.

Here are some highlights and links to the most important resources:

</span> `#ededf4` (Lavender White).

**Logo**

The logo of R-Ladies can be found in the
[branding-materials](https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/1s60cO3zRkpa-dnADPQj6w2Z1iemXH98p).

Chapter logos should use the officials colors and be designed following the instructions in the [Branding Guidelines]() and [using the regional hex logo templates]().

## Branding for presentations

Now that you know what our official colors are, you might want to use them on slides.

You can of course create your own template and share it, but feel free to re-use existing ones

- [PowerPoint and Google Slides](https://github.com/rladies/starter-kit/tree/master/templates)

- [xaringan (R Markdown!)](https://github.com/rladies/resources/blob/master/xaringan-slides/how_to_use.md)
